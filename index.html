About Flight Simulator

Flight Simulator is an immersive aviation experience that puts you in the pilot's seat of various aircraft. With realistic physics and detailed environments, you'll experience the thrill of flight from takeoff to landing.

Key Features

  • Realistic flight physics and controls
  • Multiple aircraft to master
  • Detailed environments with changing weather conditions
  • Challenging missions and free flight mode
  • Intuitive interface suitable for beginners and experienced pilots

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Use W, A, S, D keys or arrow keys to control the aircraft
  • Press Space to increase throttle
  • Press Shift to decrease throttle
  • Use Q, E for rudder control
  • Press G to deploy/retract landing gear

Tips for Success

  • Start with basic flight maneuvers before attempting complex ones
  • Practice takeoffs and landings regularly
  • Monitor your airspeed and altitude constantly
  • Adjust throttle gradually for smoother control
  • Complete tutorials to learn advanced techniques

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flight Simulator free to play?

Do I need to download anything?

No downloads required! Flight Simulator runs directly in your web browser.

What are the system requirements?

A modern web browser with WebGL support and a stable internet connection. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari on a device with at least 4GB RAM.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes, Flight Simulator is optimized for both desktop and mobile play, though the experience is more immersive on larger screens.
